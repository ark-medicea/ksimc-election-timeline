1 · EGM — Electing Electoral Commissioners (Mar–Apr)
Sun 22 Mar 77 days Cl. 6.1, 8.1
EGM notice issued
21 clear days' notice required. "Clear days" excludes both the day of notice and the day of the event, so practically 23 calendar days.
Sun 29 Mar – Sat 11 Apr · 21 clear days' notice period for EGM
Sun 12 Apr 56 days R. 7.6, 7.9
EGM held — Electoral Commissioners elected
5 commissioners elected by secret ballot (at least 1 woman, at least 1 man). Commissioners were not elected at the last AGM (27 Jul 2025), so an EGM is required before the election cycle can begin.
Mon 13 – Sun 19 Apr · Commission organises: elects Chairperson, establishes processes, requests resources from Hon Sec
2 · Pre-Election (Apr–May)
Mon 20 Apr 48 days R. 7.15
Member register snapshot date
Register of members must be correct as at 20 clear days before the Election. Hon Sec provides it to the Electoral Commission 15 clear days before.
Mon 20 Apr 48 days Cl. 18.1
Constitutional amendment deadline
Members must submit proposed amendments to the Hon Secretary at least 6 weeks (42 days) before the general meeting at which they will be considered.
Mon 27 Apr 41 days R. 7.2
Nominations open
Electoral Commission announces nominations are open and makes nomination papers available to members.
Mon 27 Apr 41 days R. 7.15
Hon Sec provides member register to Electoral Commission
Register provided 15 clear days before the Election, based on the snapshot taken at 20 clear days before.
Fri 8 May 30 days R. 7.2
Nominations close
Nomination papers must be returned to the Electoral Commission at least 14 clear days before the Election (practically 16 calendar days).
Sat 9 – Tue 12 May · Electoral Commission verifies nominations, checks eligibility
3 · Pre-AGM Notices (May)
Wed 13 May 25 days Cl. 6.1, 12.9, 18.3
AGM notice issued + audited accounts sent
21 clear days' written notice to members, specifying business to be transacted. Audited accounts must also be sent at least 21 days before the AGM. Any constitutional amendments included.
Fri 15 May 23 days R. 7.4.1
Candidate details circulated
Electoral Commission must circulate names, CVs, and manifestos of all candidates to members at least 7 clear days before the Election.
Mon 18 May 20 days Cl. 7.3
Motions deadline
Members must give 14 clear days' notice in writing to the Hon Secretary. Motions must then be circulated to members at least 10 clear days before the AGM.
Fri 22 May 16 days R. 7.4.3
Hustings (if President contested)
Mandatory if more than one candidate for President. Must be held at least 1 day before the Election.
4 · Election & AGM (May–Jun)
Sun 24 May 14 days Cl. 10.1, R. 7.16, 7.20, 7.22
🗳️ ELECTION DAY
Polling 10:30–19:30 at Imambara Building. Secret ballot. All 5 electoral commissioners must be present all day. Results declared same day. Must be on a Sunday, at least 2 weeks before the AGM.
Tue 26 May 12 days Cl. 7.3
Motions circulated to members
All motions of which notice was given must be circulated at least 10 clear days before the AGM.
Mon 25 May – Sat 6 Jun · Handover period — Incoming EC liaises with outgoing EC for orderly transfer. New EC does not become charity trustees until conclusion of AGM.
Sun 7 Jun Day 0 Cl. 6.8, 6.9, 11.4, R. 7.23
📋 AGM
Must be held between 1 June and 31 July. New Executive Committee takes office at conclusion. Accounts adopted, budget approved, subscriptions set, motions heard, constitutional amendments voted.

📝 Notes

  • "Clear days" (Cl. 20.1) — Excludes both the day of notice and the day of the event. So "21 clear days" means at least 23 calendar days between sending and the meeting.
  • AGM date constraints (Cl. 6.8) — Must be between 1 June and 31 July. Must not fall on a UK public holiday (or the Friday/Monday either side), or on an auspicious Shia Ithna-Asheri date. Sunday 7 June 2026 appears clear.
  • Election must be on a Sunday (Cl. 10.1) — Polling hours 10:30–19:30, at least 2 weeks before the AGM.
  • Uncontested positions (R. 7.4.2) — Declared elected without a vote.
  • Contested President (R. 7.4.3) — If more than one candidate, hustings are mandatory (at least 1 day before Election).
  • If no Electoral Commissioners are elected at the EGM, the entire election cycle cannot proceed.
